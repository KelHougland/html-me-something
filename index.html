Our Games

All of our games are currently in development

Circutious

Circutious is a straightforward semi-cooperative game where players attempt to build a path from start at finish together but score points individually based on the cards played.

HenchManagement

You play as middle-management henchmen aiming for an open promotion to the control room that features fewer stray bullets. Put your own henchmen to work and use your influence to complete the Dr. McEvilStein's bidding.

Dungeon Delving for Dollars

Money makes the dungeon go round. If the party makes it through the dungeon, there is a handsome reward. Players try to accumulate coins to have the biggest purse as they work their way from room to room.

Our History

Andrew and Kelly have known each other for almost 30 years. While they have toyed with the idea of making games since adolescence, it wasn't until 2014 that they set out to actually put pen to paper.

Kelly had left the area for graduate school, and Andrew's first child was fully into toddlerhood. After taking a course on designing educational games, Kelly remembered their love of games and signed them up for a local game jam. The results of which you can download below.

Several years, many trashed designs, and mind numbing amount of playtests later, our valiant heroes are just about ready to get their games into the marketplace.
